## 🚀 What I'm Building

### 🛡️ **[Secret Sanitizer](https://github.com/souvikghosh957/secret-sanitizer-extension)**  
Chrome extension that automatically detects and hides secrets (API keys, passwords, tokens) before you paste them into AI chats. Fully local, open-source, and zero telemetry.

### 🦉 **[SurpriseSage](https://github.com/souvikghosh957/SurpriseSage)**  
Your personal AI companion living in your Mac menu bar. Delivers hyper-personalized wisdom, motivation, and micro-surprises throughout the day — 100% private, runs locally with Ollama, context-aware, and never repeats itself.

### 🔒 **[Anomalyze Engine](https://github.com/souvikghosh957/anomalyze-engine)**  
Real-time AI-powered platform that detects unusual patterns in network traffic. Built for modern cybersecurity with a focus on speed, accuracy, and low false positives.
